YouTube Chapter Generator

AI-powered chapters, optimized titles, and SEO tags for educational videos

How It Works

  1. Download - Extracts audio from YouTube video
  2. Transcribe - Uses OpenAI Whisper for accurate speech-to-text
  3. Analyze - AI identifies topic introduction points
  4. Generate - Creates chapters with 4-word max titles
  5. Optimize - Generates click-worthy video titles and SEO hashtags
  6. Align - Snaps timestamps to exact transcript boundaries

Perfect For

  • Medical Education - USMLE Step 1 prep videos
  • Long Lectures - Multi-hour educational content
  • Q&A Videos - Structured question-answer format
  • Academic Content - University lectures and tutorials
